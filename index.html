ABOUT ME

As a former Pharmacist, I saw the need of technology in the industry and potential of technology to bring scalable impact to our society. I began by teaching myself some basic web skills during my free time and eventually used them to customise my e-commerce business website. The digital marketing knowledge I accumulated has led me to a position in a healthcare startup at Kickstart Accelerator 2016. And after witnessing the innovation and passion at the accelerator, I decided I wanted to be fully involved in tech.

In September 2017, I took on a 12-week Web Development Course at General Assembly where I learned all the essentials to become a full-stack web developer. It could be the satisfaction of solving problems or the ability to create and design, but I am coding day and night, and I love it!

I have been an avid gamer since I was a child. I enjoy immersing myself in fantasy worlds and sharing my experience with others through streaming on Twitch.

As a person that 'lives on the internet', I think it is pure bliss that I can do what I love for a living. My current goal is to use my new found skills to create products that improve our future.
